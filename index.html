About Me

Since launching a 20-year career in healthcare technology from microenterprise start-ups to large-scale global healthcare organizations, I have seen exceptional integration of interdisciplinary skillsets explode into revolutionary breakthroughs. At the same time, I have seen technology idle on all cylinders with silos of unparalleled talent.

My extensive experience in risk management and device development in the regulated environment offers significant opportunity to convert IT systems and coding consultants to secure software development consultants. The urgency is real, and the need is not yet being fulfilled. The landscape of medical companies, large and small, cannot be fully supported by existing resources alone. I am ready and highly motivated to build a team of trained associates to bring bleeding edge framework knowledge to the device industry.
